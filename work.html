ABOUT CONTACT WORK

Plumpy Plushies Packaging

This passion project focuses on package design for the stuffed animal company, Plumpy Plushies. Plumpy Plushies is an online store that sells a variety of stuffed animals. One stuffed animal design they’re known for are the bubble-tea-shaped plushies. Bubble tea is a Taiwanese drink that’s mixed with tea, milk, and tapioca pearls. The plushies are packaged in bubble mailer bags.

The Trap Poster

This poster was displayed at the Bachelor of Art Senior Exhibition at Louisiana State University. Photo collaging in Photoshop was used to create this aesthetic poster.

Bruce Lee Logo

The Bruce Lee logo is based on a passion project. The dragon was chosen for the logo because of its fierceness and his “little dragon” nickname. The logo is a geometric design based on the eastern dragon. Eastern dragons are snake-like, with horns and a beard. The dragon’s shape is constructed into his initials, the “B” and “L”.

Postage System

This set of stamps are based on the British Postage System. It showcases a cohesive design that reflects the “Sweet and Sour” theme. This refers to the sweetness of tomatoes and sour of pests. The bugs from left to right: fruit fly, maggot, house fly, and cockroach.
